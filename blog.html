NCNU in Spring

Enjoy drinking tea while sitting under cherry flowers Feb 4, 2019

NCNU (National Chi Nan University), located in Puli, Taiwan, now is on Cherry Blossoms Festival. Many visitors have come to this campus to enjoy this event. I had a chance to join this beautiful festival 4 years ago and interested in the way people gathered together to drink tea and chat under cherry flowers trees. Such a romatic landscape!
